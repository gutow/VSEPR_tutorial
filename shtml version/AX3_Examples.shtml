AX3 and AX2E Configurations
Molecules in this category may have three or four atoms.  The configuration is specified as AX3 for the case with four total atoms and AX2E for the case with only three atoms.  The "E" represents the electron group that is not involved in bonding.  Molecules of the type AX3 are trigonal planar and molecules of the type AX2E are bent.

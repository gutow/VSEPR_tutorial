Diatomic (Any Electron Geometries, AX)
Molecules in this category are all diatomics (molecules containing two atoms). There may be many electron groups around the "central" atom, but there is only one other atom in the molecule. The configuration is specified as AX, where A represents the one atom and the X represents the other atom.
